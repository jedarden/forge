# Control Panel Naming Options

Candidate names for the intelligent worker management system.

---

## Naming Candidates

### 1. SWARM ‚Äî Supervised Worker Agent Resource Manager
**Pros**:
- Natural metaphor (swarm of bees/workers)
- "Swarm intelligence" is well-known concept
- Short, punchy, memorable
- Evokes distributed coordination

**Cons**:
- Common in tech (many "swarm" projects)
- May imply chaotic/uncontrolled behavior
- Doesn't emphasize cost optimization

**Vibe**: Distributed, autonomous, natural

---

### 2. HIVE ‚Äî Human-in-the-loop Visibility & Execution
**Pros**:
- Strong bee/worker metaphor (pairs well with "beads")
- Emphasizes human oversight ("Human-in-the-loop")
- Short, memorable, professional
- Hexagonal structure evokes organization

**Cons**:
- Also common in tech
- Less emphasis on multi-model aspect
- "Hive mind" can have negative connotations

**Vibe**: Organized, collaborative, structured

---

### 3. FORGE ‚Äî Federated Orchestration & Review for Generative Engineering
**Pros**:
- Powerful metaphor (forging tools, weapons, solutions)
- "Generative Engineering" is modern/AI-relevant
- Implies transformation and creation
- Strong, industrial feel

**Cons**:
- "Federated" may be too technical
- Doesn't emphasize monitoring/visibility
- Slightly longer acronym expansion

**Vibe**: Creative, industrial, transformative

### 4. NEXUS ‚Äî Node Execution & eXecution Unified Supervisor

- Central hub metaphor (control center)
- Clean, modern, sci-fi feel
- "Unified Supervisor" emphasizes coordination
- Short and professional

**Cons**:
- "eXecution" capitalization feels forced
- Overused in tech/gaming
- Less unique

**Vibe**: Centralized, technological, precise

### 5. PULSE ‚Äî Parallel Unit Labor Supervision Engine
**Pros**:
- Implies life, rhythm, heartbeat

- Short and dynamic

**Cons**:
- "Labor" sounds manual/industrial
- Less memorable than others
- Doesn't convey intelligence/optimization

**Vibe**: Vital, rhythmic, monitoring

### 6. AEGIS ‚Äî Agent Execution Governance & Insight System

**Cons**:
- Less intuitive for non-classical references
- "System" is generic
- More formal/corporate

**Vibe**: Protective, authoritative, enterprise

---

### 7. FLEET ‚Äî Feedback Loop for Execution, Engineering & Tasks
**Pros**:
- Naval metaphor (fleet of ships/workers)
- "Feedback Loop" emphasizes adaptive learning
- Clean, professional
- Implies coordination and command

**Cons**:
- Common word (many fleet management systems)
- Less distinctive
- More conventional

**Vibe**: Coordinated, naval, strategic

### 8. HELM ‚Äî Human-Enhanced Labor Management
**Pros**:
- Nautical metaphor (steering/control)
- "Human-Enhanced" emphasizes AI augmentation
- Short, clear control metaphor
- Professional and authoritative

**Cons**:

- Less exciting/memorable

**Vibe**: Directive, controlled, nautical

### 9. LOOM ‚Äî Labor Orchestration & Oversight Manager
**Pros**:

- "Labor" again sounds manual
- Less dynamic than others

**Vibe**: Intricate, crafted, traditional

### 10. CAIRN ‚Äî Coordinated Agent Intelligence & Resource Network
**Pros**:
- Cairn = waypoint, guidance (hiking metaphor)
- "Intelligence" emphasizes AI/smart routing
- "Resource Network" captures distributed nature
- Unique, memorable

**Cons**:
- Less known metaphor (cairn = stone markers)
- May require explanation
- Less intuitive

**Vibe**: Guiding, exploratory, networked

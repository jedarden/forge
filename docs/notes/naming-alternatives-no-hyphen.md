## FAQ

**Q: Why ForgeLM instead of LLMForge?**
A: "Forge" as the root word is stronger for branding. "ForgeLM" sounds like a product name, while "LLMForge" sounds more descriptive/technical.

**Q: What if .com is taken?**
A: For developer tools, `.dev` is increasingly preferred. `.com` is optional backup.

**Q: Can we use just "Forge"?**
A: Too generic, trademark issues, and heavily used. Need qualifier (LM, AI, Code, etc.)

**Q: What about Forge.AI?**
A: `.ai` domains are expensive ($100+/year vs $12/year for .dev). Also "AI" is more generic than "LM".
